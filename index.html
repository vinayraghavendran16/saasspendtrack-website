FAQ

Common questions

What can SaaSSpendTrack help me track?

Everything related to your SaaS stack — vendor details, per-user-per-month costs for each tool, team-wise spend splits, pending invoices, upcoming renewals, adoption rates, and more.

How are you different from Zluri or Spendflo?

They target enterprise teams and cost $30K–$60K per year. SaaSSpendTrack gives you 80% of the visibility at a fraction of the cost, and you'll be live in 30 minutes — not 3 months.

How long does onboarding take?

Less than 30 minutes. Connect your data sources, configure alerts and workflows, and your dashboard is ready. You won't miss a single renewal or invoice.

Can I add unlimited users on the free plan?

Yes. Every plan — including Free — supports unlimited users and unlimited contracts. The only limit is the number of vendors you can track.

Is there a free trial for paid plans?

Yes. All paid plans include a 7-day free trial with full access to every feature. No credit card required to start.

How do I get support?

We offer lifetime support for all plans. Email us at support@saasspendtrack.com — we typically respond within a few hours.
